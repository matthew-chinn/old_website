Welcome to Palace

The card game of champs

Rules:

The objective of the game is to get rid of all your cards. The players start with three facedown cards and six cards in their hand. From those six cards, choose three cards (preferably the three "best" cards, which will be defined later) and place them on top of the facedown cards.

In general, players must play a card(s) with a greater than or equal to value than the previous card, on their turn. If they have multiple cards of the same value (e.g. three Kings), then they can play any number of them, provided it is a higher value than the previous card. The higher value cards are the larger ones, from a low of 3 to a high of Ace, except for the three wild cards: 2, 7, 10.

2, 7, and 10 all can be played after any previous card regardless of its value, and they have special effects. The 2 allows the player to play another card, the 7 requires the following player to play a card lower than 7, and the 10 is a "bomb", which discards all the cards in the current pile then allows the player to play again. Also, four consecutive cards of the same value counts as a bomb

After playing a card, draw a card(s) from the deck until you have at least 3 cards. If you cannot beat the current card, then you must take all of the pile into your hand. After the entire deck has been drawn and players have emptied their hands, they may use the three face up cards they had chosen at the beginning. After those, players must choose the facedown cards at random to play, and normal play continues until someone uses up all their cards
